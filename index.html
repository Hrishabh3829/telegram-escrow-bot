📌 How to Use

Step 1: Click the button above and open the bot.

Step 2: Type /start

Step 3: Choose Buyer if you are the buyer.

Step 4: Enter the seller's Telegram username.

Note: The seller must also start the bot using /start and stay online.

Step 5: Once both users are active, the seller will receive a Deal ID.

Step 6: Seller can Accept or Reject the deal.

Important: Both buyer and seller must be active in the bot for the process to work properly.
